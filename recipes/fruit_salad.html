Perfect Summer Fruit Salad

A Perfect Treat for Hot Summer Days

This fruit salad is a hit at backyard BBQs and just about any gathering—there are rarely any leftovers! It’s one of my go-to recipes because the zesty citrus dressing really enhances the flavors. For the best taste, I recommend letting it chill in the refrigerator for 3 to 4 hours so the fruit can soak up all the delicious juices.

This fruit salad is vibrant, full of color, and absolutely bursting with fresh, delicious flavor.

ingredients

Fruit

  • Pineapple
  • Strawberry
  • Kiwi
  • Banana
  • Orange
  • Grapes
  • Blueberry

Sauce

  • Orange Juice
  • Orange Zest
  • Lemon Juice
  • Lemon Zest
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

Step-By-Step

  1. Make the sauce on the stove and let it cool.
  2. Chop fruit to enjoyable size
  3. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pour the sauce over them.
  4. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flavors to meld.
