Best Hamburger Ever!

Juicy Goodness

This is the ultimate burger recipe for summer grilling! Packed with flavor and completely free of bland bread crumbs, these juicy burgers are sure to impress. Just grill them up and serve on buns with your favorite toppings and condiments.

This burger recipe delivers bold flavor, juicy texture, and total satisfaction in every bite.

ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Colby Jack
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Egg

Step-By-Step

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Make four patties
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center

Cooking!

Cooked on a grill heated to high heat, these hamburger patties should be fully cooked after about 4 to 5 minutes per side. You’ll know they’re done when the juices run clear, they’re no longer pink in the center, and an instant-read thermometer reads at least 165 degrees F.
