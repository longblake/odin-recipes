World's Best Lasagna

Lasagna Worth the Wait

Preparing lasagna may take a bit of time, but the delicious outcome is definitely worth the effort. The full list of ingredients and detailed directions are provided below, but here’s a quick overview to get you started:

This lasagna recipe is a crowd favorite thanks to its versatility—you can tweak the ingredients to match your preferences without a hitch. However, if you’d like to stick with the classic version, here’s what you’ll need to pick up at the store:

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomatoes Paste (2x)
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Egg

Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Mix ricotta and parmesan
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Layer What Matters

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan

Cooking!

Once assembled, the lasagna needs about 50 minutes in a 375°F preheated oven. Start by covering it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake uncovered for the remaining 25 minutes. Don’t forget to let it rest at room temperature for around 15 minutes before slicing—this helps it set properly.
